American Breakfast Recipes, American Veg Breakfast Recipes 

American Breakfast Recipes, American Veg Breakfast Recipes. Americans believe that busy mornings call for quick and wholesome breakfasts like cereals, pancakes or cookies, had along with coffee. Start your day with some popular options like the Spiced Wholemeal and Oats Pancake, Muesli , Muesli with Nuts , Muesli with Honey , and Buttermilk Apple Pancakes .

American Veg Pancakes for Breakfast

American Veg Pancakes for Breakfast. Pancakes is a basic breakfast option in American households like Indians love thier Idlis, Poha, Aloo Paratha for breakfast. We give you the basic eggless pancake recipe to stat with. It's easy to make and master.

Pancakes, Basic Pancake Without Eggs, Eggless Pancake

Try the Oatmeal Cinnamon Pancakes with Eggs. The addition of oats and eggs to the pancakes makes them more filling and also gives them a nice mouth-feel. A dash of cinnamon boosts the aroma and flavour of the pancakes making them truly delectable.

Oatmeal Cinnamon Pancakes with Eggs

Kids love Nutella and Strawberry pancakes. Nice and soft, these mini pancakes look dainty with the strawberry dotted Nutella topping. Make the pancakes as thin as possible so that the topping dominates the flavour. Relish it nice and fresh.

Nutella and Strawberry Mini Pancakes

Banana walnut pancakes are enriched with wheat bran, bananas and milk and are perfect for brunch. Bananas and milk provide you with all the energy needed to carry out your regular activities. They also increase the calcium and protein levels of the recipe. Most of us consider waffles and pancakes to be really sinful indulgences. It is actually the ingredients we eat with them like butter, jam or honey that actually make them full of calories.

Smoothies for American Breakfast

We present a collection of smoothies, some are super healthy to have and almost a meal by itself. The healthy peanut butter blueberry almond milk smoothie is a meal with 334 calories for the peanut butter smoothie. You cannot beat the health aspect of this and perfet for those who are lactose intolerant.

Almond Banana Smoothie

Here is a delicious Banana and Pineapple Smoothie made of fruits, low-fat milk and curds, this smoothie is a great way to include calcium-rich dairy products in your diet. The vitamin C in pineapple aids the absorption of calcium, and improves your bone mass.

American's love oatmeal for their breakfast

Oatmeal is used for breakfast in every American home. Easy to use and healthy, see benefits of oats. I cannot think of a single American hotel that does not serve some form of oats for breakfast. Try this easy banana nut oatmeal recipe where oats are cooked and banana and nuts added.

Banana Nut Oatmeal Recipe, Healthy Breakfast

The Healthy Instant Oatmeal is one of the most popular breakfast recipes across the world, all with good reason – it is healthy, easy, quick and filling! Loved by kids and adults, this no-fuss breakfast is made very easily by simply soaking oats and natural sweeteners like honey and dates in hot water. A pinch of cinnamon powder gives the oatmeal an aesthetic flavour while apple gives it volume and a fruity punch!

Healthy Instant Oatmeal Recipe, Healthy Breakfast

American's love eggs for their breakfast

American's love eggs for thier breakfast as they are fast to make and healthy. In a hurry, fried egg  and scrambled eggs is a super option. Those who love boiled eggs, there is soft boiled egg recipe and hard boiled egg recipe.
